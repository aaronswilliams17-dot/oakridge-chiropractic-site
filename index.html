FAQ

Do I need a referral?

Many patients do not. Some insurance plans may require one — we can help you check.

Is chiropractic care safe?

Chiropractic care is widely used. We screen for red flags and tailor techniques to the individual. If you have concerning symptoms (severe weakness, loss of bowel/bladder control, major trauma), seek urgent care.

How many visits will I need?

It depends on your history, goals, and how your body responds. We’ll set expectations and re-check progress.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Photo ID, insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant imaging or notes you already have.
